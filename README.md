A multi page Power BI dashboard that analyzes demographic, lifestyle, clinical, and outcome variables from the Framingham Heart Study. Advanced DAX measures and calculated columns derive actionable KPIs. Pages are designed for Executive Overview, Clinical Insights, and Risk Outcomes with slicers and diverse visuals to highlight CHD drivers such as age, smoking, cholesterol, and hypertension.


## ‚ú® Features

Executive level KPIs including total patients, CHD incidence percent, diabetes percent, smokers percent, and hypertensive percent
Clinical KPIs such as average systolic blood pressure, average diastolic blood pressure, average total cholesterol, average heart rate, average cigarettes per day, and median systolic blood pressure
Interactive slicers for sex category, age bracket, BMI group, hypertension status, smoker status, and diabetes flag
Exploratory visuals including bar charts, line charts, donut charts, pie charts, scatter plots, and radar style charts
Cohort comparisons across age groups, cholesterol categories, cigarette bands, and risk score levels

## üóÇÔ∏è Dashboard Pages

Executive Overview
A high level page with headline KPIs and core distributions including sex, age, BMI group, smoker category, and diabetic percent. Designed for quick trends and comparisons.

Clinical Insights
Charts for blood pressure by CHD, cholesterol by CHD, age against cholesterol, heart rate against BMI, BP distribution, stroke prevalence by age, and category based CHD percent views.

Risk Outcomes
Risk score distribution, CHD by risk score, outcome by age and sex, CHD by lifestyle risks, diabetic versus non diabetic outcomes, smoker versus non smoker outcomes, and BP medication distribution.
